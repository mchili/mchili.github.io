Learn About Me

Hi there! I am an energetic front-end web developer with specialties in both design and development. I value simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions that create beautiful human-centered websites. I genuinely enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser through the collaboration with UX, design, project management, and strategy teams to craft high performance and responsive websites. I am looking to deepen my web development skills as well as grow my user experience skills under the mentorship and support of my team. I currently live in the DC Metro Area working as a web developer for Social Driver, a digital agency. Take a peek at my work below or view my resume to learn more about me.

Ideas

I am versed in design thinking methodology to lead and collaborate with peers and clients. Rapid prototyping and user testing have become second nature to me. This process helps me transform ideas into human-centered design solutions.

Design

I sketch (yes, by hand!) and wireframe interfaces using detailed planning of the content structure, UI patterns and intuitive interactions with Sketch and Adobe XD. I design logos, print and digital graphic materials using Adobe Creative Suite products.

Code

I love coding, I even dream in code trying to figure out the lastest challenge. I utilize HTML, SASS, PHP and JQuery to create dynamic and responsive websites. I work with CMS systems such as WordPress and Drupal.
