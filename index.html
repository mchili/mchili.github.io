Hello! My name is Maddie Chili. I am a front-end web developer with a uncomfortable love for sweating whether boxing or moving through chaturangas at yoga. I just graduated from Elon University with degrees in computer science and communication design. I currently reside in the Washington D.C. metropolitan area. Take a peek at my work and send me an email to learn more about what I do.

Ideas

I use design thinking to cultivate collaboration with peers and clients. This process helps me transform my ideas into human-centered design solutions.

Design

I sketch (not well) and wireframe interfaces using detailed planning of the content structure, UI patterns and intutive interactions with Sketch and Adobe XD. I also design logos along with other print or digital graphic materials using Adobe Suite.

Code

I utilize HTML, CSS, SASS and a handful of JavaScript to create live websites. I have also worked with CMS systems such as WordPress and Weebly.
